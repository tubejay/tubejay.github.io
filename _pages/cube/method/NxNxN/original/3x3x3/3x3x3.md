  - CPLL
- 2-Look ELL

  - EPLL

- Cross / Advanced
- F2L
- 2-Look LLEF
  - EOLL
  - EPLL
- 2-Look L4C
  - OCLL-EPP
  - CPLL

- Cross / Advanced
- F2L
- 2-Look CPEOLL
  - EOLL
  - CPLL
- 2-Look 2GLL
  - OCLL
  - EPLL

### [Roux](/cube/method/NxNxN/original/3x3x3/roux)

- F2B
- L10P
  - CMLL
  - L6E
    - EO
    - UR/UL edges
    - M slice

### [ZZ](/cube/method/NxNxN/original/3x3x3/zz)

- EO
- Cross / Advanced
- F2L
- LL
